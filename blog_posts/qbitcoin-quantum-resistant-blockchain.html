QBitcoin

QBitcoin made an official partnership with CryptoSky Platform

QBitcoin is a next-generation quantum-resistant blockchain utilizing Falcon-512 (NIST-approved) cryptography for ultimate post-quantum security, combined with RandomX mining for fair and decentralized participation.

It solves the pressing problem of future quantum attacks that could break traditional blockchains, ensuring long-term security for transactions and digital assets.

Recently developed and technically complete, QBitcoin’s mainnet is live, miners are actively mining, transactions are happening, and it’s a fully functional working project — not just an idea. Open-source and community-driven, anyone can run a node. The project is attracting tech-savvy developers, miners, and early believers.

It supports advanced features like token creation and multi-signature wallets. The community is still small but growing steadily — with the right exposure, it’s set to expand rapidly.

ABOUT

QBitcoin is the pioneering quantum-resistant cryptocurrency built with Falcon-512 post-quantum signatures, providing unbreakable security against quantum computing attacks. Experience the future of blockchain technology with our revolutionary post-quantum cryptography implementation.

Mission

Qbitcoin was founded with a clear vision: to create a cryptocurrency that remains secure even in the face of quantum computing advancements. As quantum computers become more powerful, traditional cryptographic systems used by most cryptocurrencies face an existential threat.

The mission of Qbitcoin is to provide users with a reliable, future-proof cryptocurrency that can withstand both classical and quantum attacks, ensuring your assets remain secure for decades to come. In a world where technological advancement threatens the very foundation of digital security, Qbitcoin stands as a beacon of innovation and foresight.

Our commitment extends beyond just creating a secure currency. We aim to advance the entire field of post-quantum cryptography in practical applications, demonstrating that quantum-resistant technologies can be implemented efficiently in real-world blockchain systems today, not just as theoretical concepts for tomorrow.
